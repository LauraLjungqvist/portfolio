Why does my website need to be updated?

Signs your website is ready for an update;
  • It looks outdated.
  • It’s not responsive, or cohesive across platforms.
  • It has a bad user experience, and is hard to navigate.
  • It’s not up to date to your business strategy.
  • Content is not or cannot be updated.
  • Features are broken.

Why Wordpress?
